Common Questions

What happens if the system makes a mistake?

Every decision is logged and reversible. The system also has confidence thresholds - if it's unsure, it escalates to a human. You set the rules.

How long until we see results?

Day 1: System observes. Week 2: First recommendations. Day 30: Handling low-risk tasks. Day 90: Full autonomy on approved operations.

Is our data secure?

SOC 2 Type II certified. 256-bit AES encryption. Your data never trains our models. Full audit logs. See our security page.

What if we want to take back control?

You're always in control. Pause any module instantly. Adjust autonomy levels per function. Export all data anytime. No lock-in.

What systems do you integrate with?

500+ integrations including QuickBooks, Salesforce, Shopify, NetSuite, SAP, and most major ERPs, CRMs, and payment systems.

Do we need technical staff to manage this?

No. Ark Console is designed for operators, not engineers. Setup is guided, and our team handles the technical integration.
